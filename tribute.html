A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India

Timeline of A.P.J.Abdul Kalam

  • 1931-Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was born 15 October 1931.
  • 1954-Abdul Kalam graduated with a degree in Physics in the year 1954.
  • At Defense Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), Kalam designed a small helicopter for the Indian army
  • 1969-Kalam was transferred to ISRO in 1969 as the project director of India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III) to deploy the Rohini satellite in the earth's orbit.
  • 1990- Kalam also got honoured by the Government of India with the Padma Bhushan in 1981 and the Padma Vibhushan in 1990..
  • 1997-He has been honoured with the Bharat Ratna as well in 1997
  • 2009 In 2009, Kalam became the first Asian to be honoured with the Hoover Medal which is America's top engineering prize
  • 2015-Kalam collapsed and died from an apparent cardiac arrest on 27 July 2015, aged 83.
